F.A.Q.

What is your style of photography?
I would describe my style as natural, authentic, and timeless. I love capturing the emotions and moments that make a wedding day so special. My goal is to create images that will be treasured for generations to come.
What is your policy on rescheduling or cancelling?
I understand that life can sometimes throw us curveballs, so I try to be as flexible as possible when it comes to rescheduling or canceling.
Do you shoot destination weddings?
I love the opportunity to photograph weddings in new and exciting locations. I have experience shooting in a variety of locations and I am always up for a new adventure.
How do you deliver the images to us?
After your wedding, I will carefully edit and retouch all of your images. I will then deliver them to you in a beautiful online gallery, where you can download high-resolution digital files.
What happens if you are sick or unable to shoot my wedding?
In the unlikely event that I am unable to shoot your wedding due to illness or other unforeseen circumstances, I will do everything in my power to find a replacement photographer who is a good fit for your wedding. I have a network of professional photographers that I trust and I will work with you to make sure that your wedding is still captured beautifully.
What should we expect when working with you on our wedding day?
I believe that it is important to have clear communication and expectations when working with a wedding photographer. On your wedding day, I will be there to capture all of the special moments, from getting ready to the to the first dance and everything in between.
Do you do family photos?
In addition to engagement and wedding photography, I also offer family photography. Family photos are a great way to capture memories.
